Flappy Flip

A fun twist on the classic Flappy Bird game. Every time you score 5 points, the whole screenflips 180 making it even more challenging

and exciting!


How to Play

Press Space (or tap/click) to flap.

Avoid hitting the pipes.

Survive as long as possible and beat your high score.

Remember: every 5 points, the world turns upside down!
